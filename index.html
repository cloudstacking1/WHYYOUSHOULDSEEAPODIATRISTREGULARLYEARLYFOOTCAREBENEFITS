Understanding Podiatry: A Comprehensive Guide to Foot Health

Podiatry is a specialized branch of medicine dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and preventing conditions affecting the feet, ankles, and lower extremities. Podiatrists or doctors of podiatric medicine (DPM), are healthcare professionals who undergo extensive training to address a wide array of foot and ankle issues. Their expertise encompasses both surgical and non-surgical interventions, aiming to restore mobility and alleviate pain.?
